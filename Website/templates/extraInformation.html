Features

Sentiment Analysis based on different criteria such as:

  • Topic
  • Redditor
  • Subreddit
  • Comments
  • Domain
Once the user selected a category they can view the breakdown of the data in the next page, based on several charts and graphs. Some of those graphs are:
  • Pie Chart - Sentiment breakdown
  • Subreddit Occurence
  • Sentiment over a specific window of time
  • Wordcloud
The breakdown of the data also includes a page where you can view each specific post, its sentiment and other important information associated with it.
